A Just Internet

Rachael Tomaszewski

A Just Internet:

  1. Is Free of Socioeconomic Discrimination
  2. Attempts to Minimize Additional Privilege of the Financially Abundant
  3. Maximizes Accessibility
  4. Honors Liberty of Free Expression
  5. Prioritizes the Safety, Privacy, and Security of All Users
  6. Is a Place of Learning
  7. Is Open About What and Where Data is Stored, as well as by Whom
  8. Provides Users with the Option to Refrain from Partaking in Studies and Experiments
