Vesper's Manifesto

1.The internet was created for stronger, better connection for all of us. 2.We share knowledge. We share ideas We share secrets. 3. We have the freedom of speech. 4.However, when you see something new, get to know it before you say anything about it. 5. Remember, behind the screen, there are real people just like you. 6. Be openminded. Be kind. Be respectful.
