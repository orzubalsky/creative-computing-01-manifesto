Internet Manifesto

By Kevin Loumeau

We the people of the internet are subscribed to more than just ones and zeros.

We scroll, stream, search, and explore the world wide web every second of every day.

While tapping on a photo or tapping on a map, we are hard-wired to the crazy network called the internet.
