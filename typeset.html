Typeset Manifesto

Rachel Chang

A just internet does not have privatized ownership, nor is governed by any particular organization, but is unowned.
Every person has access, with free internet security and protection from malware, and freedom to regulate data privacy and restrict surveillance.
The internet enables otherwise small voices, to be heard.

There is intuitive UI design in every language and culture, as well as optimized language accessibility to oust lack of translation.
It is a tool for cultivation of further learning, and furtherment of a better society.
The internet should make the world smaller in terms of communication and exploration.
