Manifesto

This Manifesto has been made for the purpose of defining how a just internet should run. Be respectful of your fellow internet users. Users can control what information of theirs is displayed/hidden. Users should be well-informed of the limits of the web before using it. People of the internet should not misuse the freedom of any platform, and should be banned if so. It is our right to treat the internet right.
