A Manifesto for a Just Internet

Rachael Tomaszewski

A Just Internet:

  • Is Free of Socioeconomic Discrimination
  • Attempts to Minimize Additional Privilege of the Financially Abundant
  • Maximizes Accessibility
  • Honors Liberty of Free Expression
  • Prioritizes the Safety, Privacy, and Security of All Users
  • Is a Place of Learning
  • Is Open About What and Where Data is Stored, as well as by Whom
  • Provides Users with the Option to Refrain from Partaking in Studies and Experiments
